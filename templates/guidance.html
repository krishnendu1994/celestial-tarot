Connect with Your Angels

A simple meditation practice to connect with your guardian angels and receive their guidance.

1

Find a Quiet Space

Sit comfortably in a quiet space where you won't be disturbed for 10-15 minutes.

2

Set Your Intention

Close your eyes and set the intention to connect with your guardian angels for guidance and support.

3

Breathe Deeply

Take slow, deep breaths. Inhale peace, exhale tension. Feel yourself relaxing completely.

4

Invite Your Angels

Mentally invite your guardian angels to join you. Feel their loving presence surrounding you.

5

Ask for Guidance

Ask your angels for guidance on any situation. Listen with your heart, not your ears.

6

Receive & Thank

Receive any messages, feelings, or insights. Thank your angels and gently return to awareness.
